# Walmart Sales Forecasting & Online Retail Analysis

## Introduction
This capstone project focuses on analyzing Walmart sales data and
online retail transactions to generate business insights and
build accurate sales forecasting models.

## Problem Statement 1: Walmart Sales Forecasting
Retailers face inventory and demand planning challenges due to
seasonal variations and economic factors.
This project builds forecasting models to predict weekly sales.

### Objectives
- Analyze historical Walmart sales data
- Identify factors affecting demand
- Forecast future sales to support inventory planning

### Dataset
- Weekly sales data
- Store and department information
- CPI, fuel price, temperature
- Unemployment rate
- Holiday indicators

## Problem Statement 2: Online Retail Analysis
Understanding customer purchasing behavior is crucial for
revenue optimization and targeted marketing.

### Objectives
- Analyze customer purchase patterns
- Segment customers using RFM analysis
- Identify high-value customer groups

## Data Preprocessing
- Missing value handling
- Outlier removal
- Feature engineering
- Encoding categorical variables
- Feature scaling

## Algorithms & Models
- Random Forest Regressor
- ARIMA / SARIMA
- Facebook Prophet
- LSTM (experimental)
- KMeans clustering for customer segmentation

## Model Evaluation
Models were evaluated using:
- RMSE
- MAE
- MAPE
- Time-series cross validation

## Key Insights
- Sales peak during holiday seasons
- CPI and temperature influence demand
- Promotions introduce sales volatility
- Repeat customers contribute majority revenue

## Visualizations
- Weekly and monthly sales trends
- Correlation heatmaps
- Store-wise performance analysis
- 12-week sales forecasts per store
